# Battery warnings

There is two different kind of battery warning.

### 1./ Battery Volt

This warning is watching your batteries voltage.

You can set tis warning in CT.

![](http://gabek.ddns.net/MinimOSD-Extra_wiki_pictures/28.png)

If your battery voltage gets below the value you set, than Battery warning will show up.

### 2./ Battery Percent
This warning is watching your batteries charge level in percent.

You can set tis warning in CT.

![](http://gabek.ddns.net/MinimOSD-Extra_wiki_pictures/29.png)

If your battery capacity gets below the value you set, than Battery warning will show up.
